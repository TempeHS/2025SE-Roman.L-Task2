Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Seoul Bike Count Predictor. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our machine learning prediction service.

By accessing or using our Seoul Bike Count Predictor, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Prediction Inputs: When you use our bike count prediction service, we collect the input data you provide, such as weather conditions, temperature, humidity, wind speed, visibility, dew point, solar radiation, rainfall, snowfall, and seasonal information.

1.2 Log Data: We automatically collect certain information when you access our platform, including your IP address, browser type, device information, and timestamps of interactions.

1.3 Usage Data: We collect information about how you use our prediction service, including frequency of predictions, error reports, and diagnostic logs.

1.4 Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, maintain session integrity, and analyze usage trends.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 To provide accurate bike count predictions based on your input data.

2.2 To improve the accuracy, functionality, and performance of our machine learning models and prediction service.

2.3 To analyze usage patterns and optimize our prediction algorithms.

2.4 To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share information:

3.1 With Cloud Service Providers: We use Google Cloud services to host our machine learning models and may share prediction inputs with these services to generate predictions.

3.2 For Legal Compliance: If required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our rights and property.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, access controls, and regular audits. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Use our platform at your own risk.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

5.1 Access the data we hold about you.

5.2 Request corrections or updates to your personal information.

5.3 Request deletion of your prediction history, subject to legal retention requirements.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@seoulbikepredictor.com.

6. Retention of Data

We retain your prediction inputs and results for as long as necessary to improve our machine learning models and comply with legal obligations. When no longer required, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. Third-Party Services

Our platform uses Google Cloud AI services for machine learning predictions. We are not responsible for Google's privacy practices. Review their policies before using our service.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with the "Effective Date" updated. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the updated terms.

9. Machine Learning Model Information

Our Seoul Bike Count Predictor uses machine learning models trained on historical Seoul bike sharing data. These models analyze weather conditions and seasonal factors to predict bike rental demand. While we strive for accuracy, predictions are estimates and should not be solely relied upon for critical decisions.
